
Common Usages:
뭐 했어요? = What did you do?
뭐 하고 싶어요? = What do you want to do?
뭐라고? = What did you say?
뭐 먹고 싶어요? = What do you want to eat?
Example:
내일 뭐 하고 싶어요? = What do you want to do tomorrow?
전화번호는 뭐에요? = What is your phone number?
지난 주말에 뭐 했어요? = What did you do last weekend?
어제 학생들한테 뭐 가르쳤어요? = What did you teach the students yesterday?
