
Common Usages:
영어실력 = English ability
한국어실력 Korean ability
실력을 늘리다 = to increase one’s ability
실력이 늘다 = for one’s ability to be increased
실력이 떨어지다 = for one’s skills to go down
Example:
그 학생은 또래보다 영어실력이 뒤처지고 있어요 = That student is falling behind his peers in English ability
저의 한국어실력은 작년보다 많이 늘었어요 = My Korean skills have gotten much better compared to last year
열심히 공부한 이래로 실력은 빨리 늘었어요 = Since studying hard, my skills have been increasing
