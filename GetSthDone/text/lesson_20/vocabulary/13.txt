
Common Usages:
교수님 = professor
아버님 = a polite way to refer to one’s father
어머님 = a polite way to refer to one’s mother
따님 = a polite way to refer to one’s daughter
아드님 = a polite way to refer to one’s son
고객님 = customer
부모님 = parents
Example:
아버님과 어머님은 영어로 말씀을 못하셔요= My mother and father in law can’t speak English
The specific words for father- and mother-in-law are 장인 and 장모님, respectively. However, it is common to politely refer to them as “father” and “mother” using ~님 as shown in the example above.
